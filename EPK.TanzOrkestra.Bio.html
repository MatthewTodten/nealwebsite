Tanz Orkestra

---------------------------->

Composer Neal Todten premiered his Tanz Orkestra in 2022 to exclusively perform his extended instrumental compositions. Initially a jazz group, it was through an independently produced contemporary music series that Neal Todten began to compose in the style of contemporary Minimalist composers. The musical influences of this avant-garde style were merged with his established ensemble and became a synthesis of these two disparate styles; finding more common ground than would be expected.

The Tanz Orkestra draws on familiar instrumentation from early works of the minimalist school, utilizing multiple keyboard instruments such as electronic organ, marimba, piano, in addition to multiple wind instruments; a shared element of both minimalist and jazz styles.

Comprised of a group of seasoned New Orleans musicians, the ensemble brings a keen sense of collective improvisation to a highly rhythmic, repetition-focused music.
