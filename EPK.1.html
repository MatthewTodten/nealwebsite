House of Direction

Multi-Disciplinary Music Performance

HOUSE OF A DIRECTION is a multi-disciplinary performance experience centered around composer/artist Neal Todten’s live musical performances; exclusively featuring compositions synthesizing Minimalist musical traditions and improvisational models. Performances are framed by sweeping backdrops; large-scale painted canvases incorporating elements of cascading repetition. Original choreography for dancers visually enacts motion set forth by the music, utilizing themes of durational, repetition focused movements.


HOUSE OF DIRECTION is a highly modular performance work and can be scaled to fit a variety of venues including theaters, galleries, and outdoor spaces. Performances can include Neal Todten as a soloist or the Tanz Orkestra (3-6 musicians) under the direction of Neal Todten. Both can be accompanied by the Todten Tanzers (2-6 live dancers). Performance programs can range from 20min-90min.

::::::::::::::::::::::::::::

Neal Todten has presented work at the Happyland Theater, Music Box Village, Mudlark Public Theater, AlterSpace Gallery, and the Living Gallery – Brooklyn. He has performed with Bread & Puppet Theater, worked with La Monte Young’s Dream House, and curates an annual independent contemporary music series in New Orleans, LA where he serves as music director, composer, and producer.
