April 19 - MUSIC FOR 18 MUSICIANS @ Happyland Theater, New Orleans

MUSIC FOR 18 MUSICIANS is the fifth installment of the MINIMALISM concert series, featuring a masterwork by Steve Reich in addition to the premiere of a new work composed specifically for the occasion by Neal Todten. The concert will be hosted at the Happyland Theater located at 3126 Burgundy St. New Orleans, LA on Friday, April 19, 2024 at 8pm.
